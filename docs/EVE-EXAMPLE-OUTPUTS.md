## fullReport (premium layout) — Radiantis

The `fullReport` field uses `buildCondensedFullReport` with bridge lines, elegant labels (Signal/Ground/Reflection), and Key Moves:

```
Light Signature
How you shine when you're aligned.

Signal: Warm golden light with cool undertones, gradient between amber and soft blue.
Ground: In practice, you take in this light and hold it. You tend to pause before deciding and gravitate toward clarity. You seek spaces that let this quality breathe.
Reflection: You carry light and structure in balance—the moment you step into a room and it shifts.

Archetype
Your core pattern and how it presents.

Signal: Dominant luminous with structural undertow. Core force illumination.
Ground: In practice, you move through the world illuminating and expansive. You tend to lean toward clarity and warmth; people experience you as someone who opens doors.
Reflection: You hold illumination and structure. Light through a prism, split to color.

Deviations
Where the pattern drifts under pressure.

Signal: Gentle lateral drift with low tension and slight asymmetry.
Ground: In practice, you sometimes feel pulled sideways away from center. You tend to over-extend or drain when environments press; you recalibrate by returning to quiet.
Reflection: You bend under pressure, but the structure holds—like a branch in first snow.

Corrective Vector
How you return to center.

Signal: Pull toward coherence and rebalancing along a vertical axis.
Ground: In practice, you correct toward center. You tend to notice when you're off and take small steps back; sleep, solitude, and trusted relationships help.
Reflection: You return to your baseline—not as escape, but as home. A falling star, then stillness.

Key Moves
• step into sunlight, even for a minute
• tell one person one true thing
• open a window, let air in
```

- **Phrase bank:** sensoryMetaphors, shadowDrift, resetMoves inform imagery and tone
